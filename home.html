About Us

We are a platform-based startup that helps you connect with your FAVOURITE Stars on your special occasions. We know how much you adore your favourite YouTubers, comedians, gamers, influencers and singers and with beWishd, you can interact with them all! Hear your dearest musician sing for you on anniversary, surprise your little one with a real-time chat with his favourite superhero, play PUBG with your favourite gamer, or “beWishd” by your favourite YouTuber on your birthday!

beWishing the Community

For each request generated on our platform, we give a part of the payment away to Charity as we at beWishd want everyone’s Wish to come true!

How do we beWish you?

Generate the request

From the varied list of celebrities that are associated with us you can select your favourite star and choose the mode of interaction you would like to opt for-

  • One-to-One video or voice call
  • A real time game
  • A DM from their official accounts
  • Personalised video

Artist accepts your Wish request

Depending on their availability on the dates and feasibility, the celebrity will accept your request. Once the request is accepted you will be asked to make the payment via our portal.

Voila! You are beWishd!

On the date and time specified your favourite Artist will make your Wish come true!
